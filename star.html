⭐ Star Collector ⭐

How to Play

Click the big star to collect stars! Spend your stars on upgrades to collect even faster.

Upgrades

  • Telescope — +1 click power. Base cost: 10 stars.
  • Star Map — +5 click power. Base cost: 50 stars.
  • Space Probe — +25 click power. Base cost: 250 stars.
  • Satellite — Starts auto-collecting, then speeds it up each purchase. Base cost: 100 stars.
  • Observatory — +100 click power. Base cost: 1,000 stars.
  • Galactic Atlas — +500 click power. Base cost: 5,000 stars.
  • Star Ship — +2,500 click power. Base cost: 25,000 stars.
